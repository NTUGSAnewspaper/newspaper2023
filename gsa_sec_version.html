Living Well Being Well: The art and science of well-being

GSA welfare and NTU Welfare Service Club will hold this interactive event to help participants create greater awareness on how their personality preferences affect their well-being and how they can customize well-being tools and techniques that will support them in creating well-being more effectively for themselves and others.

 

Event Date: Monday, 06 February 2023 at 06:00 - 07:30pm

Venue: Global Lounge

Sign Up

Mindfulness Yoga Session

"You are only one yoga class away from a good mood!" Mind matters week 2023 is back! GSA welfare committee and NTU welfare service club are proud to offer you a free yoga class conducted by Yoga instructor Diana Khor. Register now and join us for a session that will leave you grounded, empowered and revitalized!

 

Event Date: Tuesday, 07 February at 7:00 - 8:30pm

Venue: Yunnan Corner Function Room

Art Analyzing Yourself

GSA welfare committee and Welfare Service Club are collaborating to organize the Art Analyzing Yourself event to encourage you to take time off for self-care and refresh through painting. We invited professional psychotherapist Stephanie Tan. She will help us release mental pressure and know our-selves better by painting. Free crayons and paper will be provided during the event!

 

Event Date: Wednesday, 08 February 2023 at 06:00 - 08:00pm

Venue: Hive TR +15

Sign Up

How to say goodbye to your comfort zone

Getting out of your comfort zone to ensure your engine is always kept warm and running. GSA welfare will hold a panel discussion as part of 2023 Mind Matters to encourage an open conversation on putting limits on us and related issues. Come and chat with our professional therapists! 

 

Event Date: Friday, 10 February 2023 at 06:00 - 08:00pm

Venue: Global Lounge

NTU Career Fair 2023*

Come to NTU Career Fair 2023, the largest recruitment fair in Semester 2! Gain an advantage in the employment market through interacting physically and virtually with hundreds of companies participating in this event, offering over 8,000 full-time graduate and internship openings! 

 

Event Date: 2, 7 & 9 Feb 2023 (10AM - 5PM); Virtual - 2 to 16 Feb 2023

Venue: NTU Nanyang Auditorium, Level B1 and Level 3

Movie Screening of "The Seeds of Vandana Shiva"*

ASE GSC, Women@NTU, and ASE Club are hosting a movie screening of "The Seeds of Vandana Shiva" on Thursday 9th February from 5.30pm-8.00pm at LT2A. This movie features Vandana Shiva, an eco-activist and agro-ecologist, and will be followed by a facilitated discussion and refreshments. Please register at the link provided!

 

Event Date: Thursday, 9 February 2023 at 5:30 - 8:00pm

Venue: LT2A at North Spine

Fully Paid Exploratory Visits to Indian Partners*

NTU India Connect office is supporting exploratory visits to Indian partner universities for the purpose of building a cohort of NTU graduate students comfortable at working in India. Visits should include the NTU supervisor plus 1-2 graduate students who will spend up to a week in India for research collaboration discussions. India Connect office shall cover all reasonable expenses. Do get your supervisor to submit nominations by 10 February 2023. Know more about India Connect here- https://www.ntu.edu.sg/about-us/global/india-connect-ntu.

 

Deadline: Friday, 10 February 2023

Sign Up

Clementi Forest Adventure*

Guided by experienced adventurers from NTU ODAC, join an adventure involving trudging over muddy streams, climbing across fallen trees and hiking through tall grasses in a natural paradise – Clementi Forest. The nominal fee is 6$(members) and 12$(non-members) for the 3.5 km long adventure trek from the Clementi MRT station to King Albert MRT station. Saturday, 18 February 2023 at 7:00-10:00am. Please register by February 16 and don't forget to put on your favorite hiking boots!

 

Event Date: Saturday, 18 February 2023 at 7-10am

Venue: Clementi-King Albert MRT Station

Event Date: From Tuesday, 17 January 2023 at 5:00 - 6:30pm

Venue: Online Zoom Meeting
